About Our Web App

Welcome to our sleep disorder prediction web application! We are passionate about utilizing technology to enhance healthcare and improve people's lives.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to provide individuals with a convenient and accessible way to assess their likelihood of experiencing sleep disorders. We understand the significant impact that sleep disturbances can have on one's quality of life, productivity, and overall health.

How It Works

Our web app utilizes advanced machine learning algorithms trained on extensive datasets of sleep patterns and associated disorders. By inputting relevant information about your sleep habits, lifestyle, and symptoms, our algorithm generates a prediction regarding the likelihood of insomnia or sleep apnea.
